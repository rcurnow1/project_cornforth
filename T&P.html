Elements of Performance

Elements of performance is a workshop that explores three key elements inherent in all perfomring and performances creation. Play, Descion Making and Reaction. Each Element is a foundation the performance is built on,

In performance and when making performance you usually have two realities. The reality within the performance and your own reality. Play can enhance this dual reality and even merge them. With these two realities you have two different sets of reactions and descisions made by your character and the one made by yourself. These two realities constantly fight for dominance. Training to become a performer or theatre maker involves being able to balance control threse three elements within the two realities
